Summary for Big Mountain’s ticket price model:

The bottom line for Big mounain’s leaders and steak holders:

Looking at the data in front of us, your profits could increase to $20,545,000 dollars by raising your ticket price to the market equivalent price of $92.74.

Currently Big Mountain charges $81.00. Our model suggests the following price: $92.74. this considers Big Mountain’s facilities and compares them to the competition. Even with our model’s possible error of $10.27 either way, there is room for a ticket price increase.

Why this will work:

Our model takes into account the facilities and prices of most of the ski resorts in the United States. With careful analysis of many different resort facilities and what they charge, we found your resort charges less for the equivalent in other resorts. We analyzed what drives prices up meaningfully, analysed those features and produced a market facility adjusted average price for your facilities.
